Cyclistic Case Study

				    
Published on 14th june, 202115 min read

			    

			    
			    

				    
				    
As the part of Capstone project in Google Data Analytics program, a case study was done on a bike-share company, to analyse their data and to derive meaningful conclusions

				    
				    
OVERVIEW

				    
Cyclistic is a bike-share company that have 5,800+ bicycles and 600+ docking stations. There are two type of riders, Annual members who take a year long membership , Casual riders who either choose for 1 ride or for the entire day. The director of the company believes comapny's futire success depends on maximizing the number of annual membership. Lily Moreno who is the director of marketing and my manager assigned me the following business task.  

				 
				    
APPROACH

				    
In this Case-Study we follow a structured 6 steps of Data Analysis as follows :

            

           
            
ASK

            
  • From 12 months of bike-sharing data, find out How do annual members and
              casual riders use cyclistic bikes differently; To Cyclistic executive team and Lily
              Moreno who are my primary and secondary stakeholders respectively
    •

  • 12 months Cyclistic's historical trip data - 1st party data.

    ANALYZE
    
          
  • The data was organized by week day
